Privacy Policy for My Web Shortcuts Extension

Last Updated: 22 Nov 2023

Welcome to My Web Shortcuts, your go-to extension for customizing keyboard shortcuts to enhance your browsing experience.

At My Web Shortcuts, we prioritize user privacy and want to ensure transparency regarding the data we collect and how it's used. Please take a moment to review the following information about our data practices:

Data Collection and Usage

  • Stored Information: The My Web Shortcuts extension currently operates with local storage and collects specific information:
    • URLs for which users have created shortcuts.
    • Shortcut objects containing keys, properties, names, and enabled statuses.
    • Additional properties associated with URLs and global extension settings.
  • Purpose: We collect this data solely to enable users to customize keyboard shortcuts for specific websites, enhancing browsing efficiency and convenience. This data remains stored locally and is editable within the extension's options page.

Data Protection and Security

We are committed to safeguarding the collected data and employ measures to ensure its protection against unauthorized access or misuse.

Contact Us

Should you have any queries or concerns regarding our privacy practices or wish to report an issue, please contact us immediately at contact@mywebshortcuts.xyz.

Changes to Privacy Policy

Our privacy policy might undergo updates or modifications. Users will be duly informed about any significant changes.

By using My Web Shortcuts, you agree to the collection and usage of data as outlined in this policy.

Thank you for choosing My Web Shortcuts to simplify your browsing experience.
